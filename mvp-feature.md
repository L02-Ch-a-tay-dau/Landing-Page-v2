Food Tinder - Discover Cuisine in a New Way
This Major Project (BTL) focuses on solving the daily "What should I eat today?" dilemma through a swipe-based interface and group decision-making features.

🚀 Core Features
The system integrates 10 key features designed to optimize the food discovery and selection process.

No.	Feature	Detailed Description	Value Proposition
1	Smart Filters	Filter by radius (Distance), price level ($ - $$$$), cuisine type (Asian, European, Vegan...), and Rating.	Helps users narrow down search results precisely based on real-time needs.
2	Restaurant Info Card	Displays high-quality food images, menu summaries, opening hours, and highlighted reviews.	Provides a quick overview without requiring multiple screen transitions.
3	Map & Navigation	View liked restaurants on a map or navigate directly via Google Maps/Apple Maps with one tap.	Optimizes travel routes and helps visualize proximity.
4	3rd-Party API Integration	Displays scores and reviews from trusted sources like Google Places, Yelp, or TripAdvisor.	Increases data credibility and provides objective perspectives.
5	Personal Collections	Save and categorize liked restaurants into custom lists (e.g., "Date Night," "Weekend Hangouts").	Helps users scientifically manage their personal culinary database.

🎯 Product Rationale
Our feature selection is driven by the goal of solving specific user pain points in their daily dining journey.

1. Focusing on Core Values: Discover & Decide
The heart of the app is supporting two critical actions: Discovery and Decision. We are not building a static restaurant directory; we are building a tool that helps users find new food and end their indecision quickly.

2. "Visual-First" Philosophy
"People eat with their eyes before they eat with their mouths."

Without vibrant food imagery and transparent information (price, atmosphere, variety), users feel hesitant. Therefore, the Restaurant Info Card is prioritized to evoke emotion and build trust from the very first glance.

3. Solving the Paradox of Choice
The biggest pain point today isn't "having nothing to eat," but "having too many choices."

Features like the Lucky Spin and Group Match turn choosing a restaurant from a stressful debate into a fun game (Gamification).
This is our core differentiator, making the app a companion rather than just a lookup tool.
4. Retention Strategy
The Personal Collections feature acts as the "hook" between the user and the app. When users invest effort into saving their favorite spots, they create a valuable personal database, significantly increasing the Retention Rate.

5. Elevating Experience with "Food Tours"
The food tour itinerary stimulates curiosity and excitement. We want to turn a standard dinner into a Micro-adventure. This increases time spent on the app and provides a unique value proposition that competitors often overlook.
